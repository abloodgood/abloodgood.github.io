Molly

Molly is a purebred Jack Russell Terrier. Jack Russell Terriers are known for their cleverness, energy, and loyalty. Molly is fiercely protective of her family but she also loves to meet new people and other dogs. She is very clever and is learning a lot during her training class. She was born March 26, 2022 in Texas.
